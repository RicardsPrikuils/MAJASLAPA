    Our expertise

    M&A sell side representation

      We seek the highest valuation for the company that the owner is selling. We assist with all stages of deal – valuation of the company, creation of a thorough information memorandum of the asset for sale, establishment of a potential buyers list both locally and globally, management of the negotiation process and assistance with legal documentation throughout the sales process.

      M&A buy side representation

        We take our customers needs in careful consideration to find the best fit to ensure organic growth and merging with other market players. We are with you every step of the way – finding the right target company, going through the due diligence process and participation in the bidding and negotiation processes. Our work ends only when the share purchase agreement is signed.

        Growth equity and debt advisory services

          We help our customers to increase leverage and unleash their companies’ full growth potential by attracting private equity funds from Baltics and broader European region, large institutional investors such as EBRD, EIF, EIB, NIB or private investors (family offices) across the Baltics, as well as help navigating through the negotiation process and settling on the best financing terms possible.

          Financial advisory services on EU grants

            We help our clients navigate through the various financing options in the market including state support programs and different grant schemes. We also take care of the submission of all documentation necessary for the application to such grants.

            Capital investments

              We consider capital investment opportunities by investing in them ourselves, as well as by attracting local enterpreneurs, holding companies and foreign investors. We assist with both strategic long-term investments and medium-term financial investment projects.
