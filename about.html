    About us

    We provide M&A deal expertise and independent financial advisory services to our clients in the Baltic countries and the broader Central and Eastern European region.

    Our office has existed since 2016 and during these years we have successfully implemented several dozen client projects - both sales of companies and attraction of large-scale financing. We have also worked as financial consultants on the first Public-Private Partnership or PPP project in Latvia.


    Entrepreneurs who seek growth, expansion or mergers to become sizeable players in the market. Owners who wish to sell their companies. Private equity firms who look for new exciting opportunities in the region. Global corporations who want to expand further. Whichever group you belong to, we are here to help you make these dreams a reality.
